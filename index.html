About me

I live in Bulgaria and currently I am student in Software University. Coding was not something I imagined myself doing about 2 years ago. Now its something that I do everyday and enjoy very much. With every new project I like to do everything differently, that helps me constantly learn new things. I gained a lot of experience with responsive design which lets me optimise your web page for every device and the various browsers. My focus is directed to clean and modern design that is both interactive and easy to use for everyone.
